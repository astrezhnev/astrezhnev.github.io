## DirectEffects

An R package estimating controlled direct effects by fixing potential mediators to specific values. Implements sequential g-estimation and telescope matching estimators.

**Programming Language:** R

**Repository:** [GitHub](https://github.com/mattblackwell/DirectEffects)

**Authors:** Matthew Blackwell, Avidit Acharya, Maya Sen, Shiro Kuriwaki, Jacob Brown, Anton Strezhnev

**Associated Publications:**
- Vansteelandt (2009)
- Acharya, Blackwell & Sen (2016)
- Blackwell & Strezhnev (2022)
